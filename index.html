OUR MISSION


We focus on doing our job so you can do yours. When you call Carper Casket Company you get a Carper, which is a family tradition of service.



OUR BUSINESS


For our funeral home clientele, we focus on providing innovative, practical, and excellent funeral related products that reflect quality craftsmanship. We specialize in reasonable metal and oversize caskets. We support American made products and vendors. Our caskets are not out-sourced from other countries. We buy from nationwide manufacturers who employ American laborers. We will continue to provide business strategies to slash cost for our customers.
